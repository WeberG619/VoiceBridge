Installation

1 Download from Google Play Store

Search for "VoiceBridge" in the Google Play Store or click the download link on our website.

2 System Requirements

Ensure your device meets the minimum requirements:

  • Android 7.0 (API level 24) or higher
  • 2GB RAM minimum (4GB recommended)
  • 1GB free storage space
  • Microphone access
  • Camera access (optional, for document scanning)

Granting Permissions

Important: VoiceBridge needs specific permissions to function properly. All permissions are used only for core features and never for data collection.
üé§

Microphone Permission

Required for voice input and form filling

Your voice is processed locally and never stored or transmitted.

üì∑

Camera Permission

Optional for document scanning and OCR

Images are processed locally and immediately discarded.

‚ôø

Accessibility Service

Required for automatic form filling

Allows VoiceBridge to interact with form fields in other apps.

1 Grant Basic Permissions

When prompted, grant microphone and camera permissions. You can always change these later in Settings.

2 Enable Accessibility Service

This is the most important step:

  1. Tap "Grant" when prompted for accessibility service
  2. You'll be taken to Android's Accessibility settings
  3. Find "VoiceBridge" in the list
  4. Toggle it ON
  5. Confirm when prompted
  6. Return to VoiceBridge

Your First Form

Ready to try! Let's fill your first form with VoiceBridge.
1 Open Any Form

Navigate to any form in your browser or app. Good examples:

  • Contact forms on websites
  • Registration forms
  • Survey forms
  • Government applications
2 Activate VoiceBridge

Look for the VoiceBridge microphone button that appears as an overlay. If you don't see it:

  • Pull down your notification shade
  • Tap the VoiceBridge notification
  • Or open VoiceBridge and tap "Start Form Filling"
3 Speak Naturally

Tap the microphone and speak naturally:

"My name is John Smith, email john@example.com, phone 555-123-4567"

VoiceBridge will automatically identify and fill the appropriate form fields!

Tips & Tricks

üí° Voice Commands

Use these helpful voice commands:

  • "Next field" - Move to the next form field
  • "Clear field" - Clear the current field
  • "Submit form" - Submit the completed form
  • "Stop listening" - Stop voice recognition
üì± Best Practices
  • Speak clearly and at a normal pace
  • Use a quiet environment for better accuracy
  • Hold your device 6-12 inches from your mouth
  • Pause briefly between different pieces of information
  • Review filled fields before submitting
‚ö° Efficiency Tips
  • Group similar information together when speaking
  • Use the document scanner for pre-written information
  • Create custom skills for frequently filled forms
  • Enable quick access from the notification panel

Troubleshooting

Having issues? Here are solutions to common problems.

üé§ Voice Not Recognized

  • Check microphone permissions in Settings > Apps > VoiceBridge
  • Ensure you're in a quiet environment
  • Speak clearly and not too fast
  • Check if your language is supported
  • Restart the app if issues persist

üö´ Form Fields Not Filling

  • Ensure Accessibility Service is enabled
  • Check if the app/website is supported
  • Try switching to manual field selection mode
  • Some forms may require manual submission

üì∑ Camera/OCR Issues

  • Ensure good lighting when scanning
  • Hold the camera steady and close enough to read text
  • Clean your camera lens
  • Try different angles if text isn't detected

üîß General Issues

  • Restart VoiceBridge
  • Check for app updates in Google Play Store
  • Ensure you have sufficient storage space
  • Restart your device if problems persist
